Our Services

Tremix Flooring

Tremix Concrete Flooring is a high-strength, vacuum-dewatered flooring system designed for durable, smooth, and dust-free surfaces. It is widely used in industries, warehouses, parking areas, and heavy-load zones for its long life and superior performance.

Learn More

Stampcrete

Stampcrete Flooring is a decorative concrete technique that creates natural stone, brick, tile, or custom textured finishes on concrete surfaces. It is durable, slip-resistant, and ideal for driveways, walkways, parking areas, gardens, and outdoor spaces, offering both strength and attractive design.

Learn More

Groove Cutting

Groove cutting and filling is done after concrete sets to control cracking and extend floor life. Precision grooves act as control joints, allowing natural movement without random cracks. These joints are cleaned and filled with bitumen or polysulphide sealant to block dust, water, and edge damage, ensuring a stronger, more durable concrete floor.
